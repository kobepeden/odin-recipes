Orange and Lime Margarita Recipe!

Description

I've been searching for the perfect drink to sip by the pool and relax, until I finally came across this perfect margarita mixture. Look no further!

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 oz tequila
  • 1 tbsp agave syrup
  • ice as needed
  • orange twist

    • Steps:

    1. Pour orange juice, lime juice, tequila, and agave syrup into a cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake well. Strain into margarita glass and serve with an orange twist.
