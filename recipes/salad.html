Chicken Bacon Ranch Salad!

Description

Now it seems that our cookbook is missing something a bit on the healthier side, so here we have the perfect chicken bacon ranch salad. It's simple and easy, and you never get tired of it!

Ingredients:

  • 8 leaves romaine lettuce
  • 2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup chopped cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup chopped avocado
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tbsp prepared ranch dressing
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 slices cooked bacon, crumbled

    • Steps:

    1. Line a salad plate with small romaine lettuce leaves.
    2. Combine chopped romaine, chicken avocado, and tomatoes in a large bowl. Add ranch dressing, salt, pepper, and toss to combine.
    3. Serve salad on the prepared plate, top with crumbled bacon.
